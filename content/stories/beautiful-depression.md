---
title: Beautiful Depression
date: 2026-01-11T11:11:00.000-05:00
---


He loved her. She loved him.

A beachside date. 11 PM. Waves lapping at the shore.

Her brown eyes. Her light brown hair.

He brings his hand through her hair. She smiles up at him. He puts his arm around her.

They lean back and stare out over the ocean. They fall asleep in each other's arms.

They wake up just in time for the sunrise.

“Its almost as beautiful as you are.” he says.

She leans in. He leans in.

Then he wakes up.
